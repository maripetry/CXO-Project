Context & Challenges

The FBA Opportunity

First Bank of Australia is a full-service bank with $12.6M retail customers and 900K SME clients across Europe, North America, and the recently acquired Brazilian operations. The strategic acquisition in Brazil positions FBA to capitalize on the LATAM market and use this region as an incubator for global transformation.

Business Transformation: 4 Pillars

  • Scale & Growth: Expand in Europe/North America and drive new growth in LATAM with agile market response
  • Macroeconomic Adaptation: Respond to changing customer demand and economic forces impacting revenue and profitability
  • AI & Innovation: Adapt to customer expectations driven by emerging technologies and competitive alternatives including embedded finance
  • Customer Experience: Deliver responsive, personalized, adaptive journeys across all channels and markets
  • Value Maximization: Focus on high-potential segments (SMEs, Gen Z) with expanded product consumption
  • Organizational Transformation: Shift to customer-centric culture with enhanced security, compliance, and risk management

Current Business State

FBA achieved strong financial growth in the previous fiscal year with excellent financial risk management ratings. The organization has completed its 5-year business plan with ambitious targets to significantly accelerate income growth and nearly double profitability. The Brazilian market presents a highly digitized environment with open finance regulations—a fierce competitive landscape that demands rapid innovation.

Salesforce Foundation in Brazil

Banco FBA already operates Salesforce Financial Services Cloud with 530 users in Call Centre and Credit Card Operations, plus 25 Marketing users. Marketing Cloud powers personalized offer management, and Mulesoft integrates legacy systems. Current licence value: $650K (agreement ends January 2027).

Why Salesforce

Strategic Partnership vs. Vendor Relationship

FBA's CXOs recognize that transformation success depends on more than software. They seek a strategic partner who can:

  • Minimize execution risk across complex, multi-geography transformation
  • Drive business outcomes aligned with the 4 Pillars strategy
  • Provide ongoing innovation and competitive advantage

Salesforce Enables the 4 Pillars

1️⃣ Scale & Growth

Financial Services Cloud and Service Cloud provide enterprise-scale architecture to manage millions of customers across geographies, seamlessly integrating with open finance requirements and enabling rapid expansion into new markets.

2️⃣ Macroeconomic Adaptation

Salesforce's AI-powered analytics (Einstein) and real-time dashboards enable rapid insights into customer behavior changes, market trends, and profitability drivers—enabling agile response to economic forces.

3️⃣ AI & Innovation

Einstein AI, embedded in all Salesforce clouds, enables next-generation capabilities: predictive analytics, intelligent recommendations, chatbots, and embedded finance connectors. Salesforce AppExchange provides pre-built fintech innovations.

4️⃣ Customer Experience

Unified Customer 360 creates adaptive journeys across all channels. Personalization Engine delivers contextual experiences. Service Cloud, Commerce Cloud, and Marketing Cloud orchestrate seamless omnichannel interactions.

5️⃣ Value Maximization

Segment-specific solutions for SMEs and Consumer Banking, combined with advanced targeting and propensity analytics, enable focus on highest-value customer segments and expanded product consumption.

6️⃣ Organizational Transformation

Salesforce's customer-centric platform drives cultural change. Robust security, compliance, and privacy controls address regulatory challenges. Global delivery network and partner ecosystem ensure execution excellence.
